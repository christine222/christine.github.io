Rainbow Machine

May 2016
An skittle sorting mechanism powered by an Arduino Uno. Sorts skittles based on color by repeatedly flashing an rgb led onto a skittle and using a phototransistor to read the intensity of light reflected off of the skittle. Based on the intensity of light, two servos work in conjunction to sort the skittles into small cups.
